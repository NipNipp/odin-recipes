Nutella Toast on a Plate

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread of any kind
  • Jar of Nutella
  • Butter is optional
  • Butter knife
  • Toaster

Steps

  1. Place sliced bread in toaster and toast it to desired lenght.
  2. Once toasted, spread butter (optional) onto toast.
  3. Spread Nutella onto toast.
  4. Eat the toast.
